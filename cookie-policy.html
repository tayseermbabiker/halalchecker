What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help us provide you with a better experience.

How We Use Cookies

Essential Cookies: Required for basic site functionality like navigation and secure areas.

Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how visitors use our site through Google Analytics.

Advertising Cookies: Used to show relevant ads through Google AdSense and other partners.

Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences.

Third-Party Cookies

We use third-party services that may set their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics (website traffic analysis)
  • Google AdSense (advertising)
  • Social media platforms (sharing buttons)

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect site functionality.

Most browsers allow you to:

  • See what cookies are stored
  • Delete some or all cookies
  • Block cookies from specific sites
  • Block all third-party cookies
