Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Note: This is a template privacy policy. Please review and customize with proper legal language before going live.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Email address when you subscribe to our newsletter
  • Name and contact information when you submit the contact form
  • Comments and feedback you provide

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Send you our weekly Islamic parenting newsletter
  • Respond to your inquiries and requests
  • Improve our website and content
  • Analyze website usage through analytics tools

3. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve your experience. This includes:

  • Google Analytics for understanding website traffic
  • Essential cookies for website functionality
  • Third-party cookies from advertising partners

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information:

  • Google Analytics for website analytics
  • Google AdSense for advertising
  • Email service providers for newsletters
  • Social media platforms for sharing content

5. Email Communications

When you subscribe to our newsletter:

  • We will send you weekly Islamic parenting tips and resources
  • You can unsubscribe at any time using the link in our emails
  • We will never sell or share your email with third parties for marketing

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is intended for parents and adults. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Opt-out of marketing communications

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting a notice on our website.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at:

Email: hello@halaldetect.com
