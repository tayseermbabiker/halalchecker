Frequently Asked Questions

How do I submit a parent question?

Use the contact form above and select "Content Suggestion" as the subject. Share your parenting challenge or question, and we may feature it in an upcoming article (anonymously, of course).

Can I write for HalalDetect?

Yes! We welcome guest contributors who have expertise in Islamic parenting, education, or child development. Visit our Write For Us page for submission guidelines, or contact us with "Partnership Inquiry" selected.

Do you offer sponsored content or advertising?

We do work with brands and organizations that align with our values and serve the Muslim community. For advertising inquiries, please select "Partnership Inquiry" in the contact form above.

How do I report an issue with the site?

If you encounter a technical problem, broken link, or other site issue, please use the contact form and select "Technical Issue" as the subject. Include as much detail as possible about what went wrong.
